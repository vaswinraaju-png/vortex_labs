What Is Creative Fatigue and Why Does It Matter in 2026?

Creative fatigue occurs when your audience sees the same ad creative too often, leading to ad blindness and declining engagement. In 2026, with over 2.35 billion people reached daily by Meta's ads (facebookcapi.com), the competition for attention is fiercer than ever. If you're not addressing creative fatigue, you're losing significant revenue opportunities.

What You Lose by Ignoring It

Ignoring creative fatigue results in increased CPLs and diminished ROAS. For example, in the beauty industry, where CPAs range from $32–$68 and ROAS averages 2.2–4.0x (growwithba.com), failing to refresh creatives timely could see your ROAS plummet below industry averages. With CPCs rising 10–25% across the board (foundrycro.com), every wasted impression compounds your loss.

Regularly rotating ad creatives keeps them fresh. Aim for a rotation every 7-10 days, depending on performance feedback.

How Do Performance Marketers Avoid Creative Fatigue?

Creative fatigue is the silent killer of Facebook ad campaigns. With a mere 8.4-day lifespan, creative assets need frequent refreshing (adriselab.com). Ignoring this can drastically lower engagement rates and inflate costs.

Staying Fresh

1. Audit Regularly: Check ad performance daily to spot signs of fatigue early.

2. Plan Rotations: Have a stable of ready-to-deploy creatives to switch in and out.

3. Test Variations: Use different formats, messages, and visuals to keep users engaged.

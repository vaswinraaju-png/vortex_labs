Why Are 73% of Media Buyers Overlooking Ad Frequency?

Ad frequency is a silent killer of campaign performance. Too high, and you risk annoying your audience; too low, and your ads might not be effective.

Optimal Frequency Strategies

  • Monitor your ad frequency to keep it between 1.8 to 2.5 impressions per user.
  • Adjust your bidding strategy accordingly.

Consequences of High Frequency

Neglecting this can lead to ad fatigue, negatively impacting your CTR and ultimately your ROAS.

How to Improve ROAS by 2.3x Using Ad Creative Refreshes?

Ad creative plays a pivotal role in engagement. Stale visuals can drag your campaign down.

Regular Creative Updates

Change your ad creatives every 2-3 weeks to keep the content fresh and engaging.

A/B Testing

Continuously A/B test your creatives to identify what resonates the best with your audience.

Visual Impact Analysis

Creatives that are refreshed regularly can boost ROAS by 2.3x, ensuring you get the most out of every dollar spent.

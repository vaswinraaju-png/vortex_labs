FAQs

Q: What is the Facebook Ads Library?

A: It is a platform that provides transparency of ads running on Facebook, accessible to anyone for analysis.

Q: How does Facebook Ads Analysis improve ROAS?

A: By uncovering competitor strategies, it helps refine your ad approach, leading to better-targeted and more engaging campaigns.

Q: Can I see how much my competitors spend on ads?

A: While exact spend isn't detailed, the library offers insights into the types and frequency of ads they run.

Q: What should I focus on when analyzing competitor ads?

A: Pay attention to creative elements, messaging, audience targeting, and engagement metrics.

Q: How often should I perform a Facebook Ads Analysis?

A: Regularly monitor your competitors' ads and adjust your strategies at least monthly to stay competitive.
